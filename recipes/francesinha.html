Francesinha

Description

The Francesinha is a typical dish from northern Portugal, in the Porto region.

It is a very greasy but nonetheless delicious dish!

Ingredients

  • 3 slices of bread
  • a cow steak
  • 4 slices of cheese
  • 1 slice of ham
  • 1 sausage
  • 1 egg
  • olive oil
  • francesinha sauce
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the sausages horizontaly in half and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Toast the bread slices and put one in a plate, then place the steak on top, a ham slice, followed by another slice of bread and finally the sausages.
  3. Put another slice of bread on top of the sausages and put the slices of cheese on top.
  4. Put in the oven at 200ÂºC until the cheese melts.
  5. Put the francesinha sauce on top and it is ready to serve!
