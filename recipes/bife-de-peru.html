Bife de Peru

Description

Bife de Peru isn't really an original portuguese recipe per se, but it has become a very common dish in Portugal.

It is easy to understand why, portuguese people love sauces!

Ingredients

  • 4 turkey steaks
  • 250g of fresh mushrooms
  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 250ml of cream
  • 2 spoons of butter
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. On a frying pan, start heating half of the butter until it melts. Add the turkey steaks seasoned with salt and pepper and cook them. Remove when done.
  2. On the same frying pan, add the other half of the butter and add the chopped onion and garlic cloves and saute. Add the cut mushrooms until they cook.
  3. Add the cream and season with salt and pepper. Finally add the steaks to the frying pan again and mix everything!
